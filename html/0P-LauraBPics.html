Finally we have a picture of Marguerite, between Chris and G. O. Marie stands to Chris's right. The youngest girl in front is Geraldine, dating this photo to about 1931.

It is also likely that the picture documents a 1932-09-09 Clifton Record entry: Mr. G. O. Bronstad had as his guests Sunday morning two of his old friends, Dr. John Murphy of Temple and Dr. Jim Barnell of Johnson city. These two men were once residents of this city.
