Petra Foss (daughter of Johannes Olsen Vestrum Foss and Berte Marie Pedersdatter Aasmundred) was born March 10, 1845 in Vestre, Norway. She came with her parents to Four Mile Prarie in Van Zandt County, Texas. She wed Hans in Four Mile. The family moved to Bosque county around 1870.

Hans Jorgen Hansen, a Danish immigrant wed Petra Foss an Norwegian immigrant in Van Zandt County, TX.
