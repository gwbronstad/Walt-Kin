Friends Visit

Finally we have a picture of Marguerite, between Chris Rohne and G. O. Bronstad. Leftmost, by Chris is his wife, Marie (Bronstad) Rohne. The young girl in front of Laura is LoVerne Rohne, dating this photo to about 1932. Rightmost in the picture is Clyde Bronstad, standing beside his Mother Laura.

It is also likely that the picture documents a 1932-09-09 Clifton Record entry: Mr. G. O. Bronstad had as his guests Sunday morning two of his old friends, Dr. John Murphy of Temple and Dr. Jim Barnell of Johnson city. These two men were once residents of this city. They may also be the tall men most hidden in the photograph, their wives and children competing the picture.
