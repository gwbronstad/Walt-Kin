Mother and Father of Gulbrand Olson Bronstad

Gulbrand followed his brother Christian Bronstad's example and gift of a $10 gold coin to come to the United states at about age 18 in 1874. This picture was from the 1880's, maybe from the time Gulbrand was in Morgan, Texas, learning the storekeeper's trade at Miller Merchandising Store.

It took Gulbrand (born March 9, 1856) a while to find a wife. Gulbrand and Laura's first child, Otis Julian, was born September 26, 1894, Christian (born August 20, 1853) and Martha's 10th child, Alvin Lawrence, was born March 18, 1894 at Norse, TX. (Alvin Laurence Bronstad did a wonderful job of recording his family's history.)

This could be around the time In 1904, G. O. and Laura Bronstad enjoyed several days at the World's Fair in St. Louis.

Proud family man and merchant with freshly painted home, worth sending a copy to his brother back in Norway.

Homer Hansen Bronstad, born July 17, 1907, looks about 2 years old in his father's arms. This photo must date to 1909. Standing in back are Otis, G. O., and Clyde.In front are Marie, Lorine, Laura Wilhellmina Hansen Bronstad and woman unknown

This could be around the time in 1910 when Gulbrand helped establish the First Guaranty State Bank at Cranfills Gap.

Many businesses were moved about 700 yards to the northeast.

Friends Visit

Finally we have a picture of Marguerite, between Chris Rohne and G. O. Bronstad. Leftmost, by Chris is his wife, Marie (Bronstad) Rohne. The young girl in front of Laura is LoVerne Rohne, dating this photo to about 1932. Rightmost in the picture is Clyde Bronstad, standing beside his Mother Laura.

It is also likely that the picture documents a 1932-09-09 Clifton Record entry: Mr. G. O. Bronstad had as his guests Sunday morning two of his old friends, Dr. John Murphy of Temple and Dr. Jim Barnell of Johnson city. These two men were once residents of this city. They may also be the tall men most hidden in the photograph, their wives and children competing the picture.

Picture from Christian O. Bronstad's Funeral

The front row contains Christian Olson Bronstad's siblings and his widow. The woman behind Gulbrand is Georgie Bronstad, wife of Alvin Lawrence Bronstad.
