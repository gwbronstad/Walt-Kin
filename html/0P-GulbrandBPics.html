Pictures of Gulbrand Olson Bronstad

I have but few pictures from his life. Click on any picture to see it enlarged.

Gulbrand followed his brother Christian Bronstad's example and gift of a $10 gold coin to come to the United states at about age 18 in 1874. This picture was from the 1880's, maybe from the time Gulbrand was in Morgan, Texas, learning the storekeeper's trade at Miller Merchandising Store.

It took Gulbrand (born March 9, 1856) a while to find a wife. Gulbrand and Laura's first child, Otis Julian, was born September 26, 1894, Christian (born August 20, 1853) and Martha's 10th child, Alvin Lawrence, was born March 18, 1894 at Norse, TX. (Alvin Laurence Bronstad did a wonderful job of recording his family's history.)

This could be around the time In 1904, G. O. and Laura Bronstad enjoyed several days at the World's Fair in St. Louis.

Homer Hansen Bronstad, born July 17, 1907, looks about 2 years old in his father's arms. This photo must date to 1909. Standing in back are Otis, G. O., and Clyde.In front are Marie, Lorine, Laura Wilhellmina Hansen Bronstad and woman unknown

This could be around the time in 1910 when Gulbrand helped establish the First Guaranty State Bank at Cranfills Gap.

Many businesses were moved about 400 yards to the east.

Finally we have a picture of Marguerite, between Chris and G. O. The youngest girl in front is Geraldine, dating this photo to about 1931.
