Bronstads and Waltons toured the West.


A Jobian year of death took Will Walton, Embrey Walton Jr. and Pansy Walton. The year following, Ethel Walton, Clyde Bronstad, Embrey Walton, Louise Bronstad, Nan Walton, Walt Bronstad and Bette Bronstad piled into the '56 Pontiac station wagon (loaned by Annie Joy Walton) and Nan's new '57 Chevrolet. We stopped at the Petrified Forest, Hover Dam, the Grand Canyon and Aubrey Gilkes house in Fresno California.

With Aubrey as guide we saw Stanford and Berkeley universities and Yosemite National Park.
