She ran quickly into the house. The others questioned each other with silent glances. When she came into the dining room from her room, they gathered around her. In her hands she held her precious box. Quickly she pushed it into Aunt Nell's hands.

"Look in it!" she said softly.

"A bird's nest" Aunt Nell said. Then she gasped. For woven in and out of the nest was a string with a few blue beads on it. On one side ot the nest hung one bead more beautiful than the rest It was the much loved center bead.

(bird's nest)

Linda didn't wait to be asked "I found it in the hackberry tree, and on the ground I found—Look—many of the other beads that were lost from the string. See, they're in the box!"

Aunt Nell said no more. She just took Linda into her arms.
