Darkness was coming fast now. A light was turned on in the house. Linda was almost asleep when she heard someone near her.

"Why Linda, Dear! Why didn't you come in? We didn't know you were here."

Was she dreaming? It was Aunt Nell!

"Aunt Nell!" Linda's arms went around her neck. "I thought you went home!"

"Well, surely you would have come in to see me if you had known I was here!" Aunt Nell teased.

But wasn't it your car I saw leave?" Linda asked.

I had to send my car to town, too. That changed my plans. I can stay until tomorrow afternoon."

"Oh Aunt Nell! Wait!" Linda seemed to have a new surge of eagarness. "Wait here just a minute."
