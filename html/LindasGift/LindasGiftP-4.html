(Horses Peg and Mark)

Soon she was driving the three gentle horses Peg, Mark, and Beck toward the field. As she jumped lightly over the small stream, her eyes searched for fish. Only seldom did she see one, but she always looked.

(small stream fish)

As she followed Father and his four horses up the gently slanting hill, the cleanness of the air the blue of the sky seemed to reach her with a new freshness.

(two wildflowers)

Only a few flowers were blooming, but they tried to out-glow one another. The dew added to their brilliance. She remembered a word her mother had taught her and said to herself, "I feel exhilarated. This wonderful secret!"
