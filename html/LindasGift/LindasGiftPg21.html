She looked down the hill at the little gray pond where she loved to fish. This mothing it had looked like a little grey kitten curled up the the feet of the willow trees beyond it. Yesterday durig a strong wind she had seen the willlows tugging at their roots as though they wanted to free themselves from the ground. Today they were gently yielding to the slight wind.

Today as she looked at the flat gray water she thought, "You have a dirty look on your face."
