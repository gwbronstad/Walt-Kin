Linda had made many special searches. If only she could be the one to find the beads! She remembered the tender, sad look that came to Aunt Nell's eyes as she spoke of the beads. In her last few letters to Mother, Aunt Nell had not mentioned them.

A meadow lark's song brought Linda back to earth. They were nearing a clump of trees. She looked up quickly and saw the lark sitting on the wire fence. As the plow neared, the lark flew away.
