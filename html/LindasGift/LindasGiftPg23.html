Small dark clouds covered the sun once in a while. They made a welcome shadow. Soon a larger dark cloud covered the sun. Rain drops came, large and far apart.

Father called back, "It's just a light shower. It will soon be over. Let's go ahead with our work."

Father was right. It was over in a few minutes. Linda knew her jacket would dry quickly in the sunshine that would return.

The rounds were quite short now and the sun's rays were much kider. At last when the sun was low in the sky, Father stopped his horses.

"Well, we did not get through, but it won't take me long in the morning to finish this. Let's go home, Linda."

When they reached the barn Father sent Linda to the house. You're tired, honey. I'll feed your horses."
