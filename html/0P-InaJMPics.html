Draw a card left to right across picture; faces first encountered are: William Twyman Walton, Louise Myrtle Walton, Frederick Cook Walton, Aubrey Irene Walton, William Embrey Walton, Ethel Irene (Embrey)(Price) Walton, Ina Jo Walton and Annie Joy Walton.
Little Frederick, at his father's knee, died in a farming accident before age 6, May 5, 1915.

They're all dressed differently, as if needing to move on past their collective grief over the loss of Frederick. Draw a card left to right across the picture, faces touched first are: Will, Louise, Annie Joy, Ina Jo, Embrey, Ethel and Aubrey.

This photo was made near the time of The Spanish Flu; Ethel may have been recovering.
