1910

Louise born 1908-04-23, died 1989-08-18. Frederick born 1909-09-22, died 1915-05-05 as the result of a farm work accident. Travelling back home with his uncle after a day of hay bailing, a telephone line caught the handle of the heavy hay press. Frederick had been sitting beside it; he jumped out of the wagon first, the hay press came down next, crushing Frederick.

The event was on or near 17th of December 1951. Aubrey likely could not break free from work. The venue was a Hamilton restaurant.
