Mark 001



Lorine's Fatther, Gulbrand was a Norwegian immigrant. Gulbrand followed his brother, Christian Olson Bronstad, to the United States after Christian had become established in these United States.


Lorine's Mother, Laura was first generation American, her Mother, Petra (Foss) Hansen Was born in Vestre Norway and Immigrated with her Father, Johannes Foss, and Mother, Berta Foss, to Four Mile, TX. Laura's Father, Hans Jorgen Hansen, was an immigrant from Denmark.




Lorine item 001

1932-04-01

Birth of Sue Hudson

Word has been received here that Mr. and Mrs. Douglas Hudson of Ada, Oklahoma are the proud parents of a nice baby girl named Carolyn Sue, born March 20. Mrs. Hudson was formerly Miss Lorine Bronstad daughter of Mr. and Mrs. G. O. Bronstad of this city.

1932-06-03

Mrs. Douglas Hudson and daughter, Carolyn Sue of Ada, Oklahoma, came in Sunday to visit with her parents, Mr. and Mrs. G. O. Bronstad. . . .

>

1933-02-10

Mr. and Mrs. Douglas Hudson and little baby, Sue, came in Friday from Ada, Okla., to visit in the home of her parents, Mr. and Mrs. G. O. Bronstad, and other relatives and friends. Mrs. Hudson will be remembered as Miss Lorene Bronstad and her friends can only be counted by those who know her. . . .

1933-02-24

Mr. and Mrs. Douglas Hudson and little baby, Sue, who have been visiting in the home of her parents, Mr. and Mrs. G. O. Bronstad, for the last two weeks, returned to their home in Ada, Okla., last Monday, going by way of Waco. Her father accompanied them to Waco.

1934-04-27

Mrs. Douglas Hudson and charming little daughter, Carolyn Sue of Ada, Oklahoma are on an extended visit with relatives here.

1934-08-17

Mr. and Mrs. Chris L. Rohne spent the week-end in Austin with Mr. and Mrs. Homer Bronstad. Mr. and Mrs. Rohne left Wednesday morning for Oklahoma where they will visit with Mr. and Mrs. Douglas Hudson and daughter. Chris is in his vacation, you know.

1934-09-21

Mr. and Mrs. Homer Bronstad, Mr. and Mrs. G. O. Bronstad and Miss Marguerite Bronstad spent a few days last week in Oklahoma visiting with Mr. and Mrs. Douglas Hudson,

1935-07-05

Mr. Douglas Hudson of Holdenville, Oklahoma, came to join his wife and daughter who are spending their vacation here. They will all go back to their home in Holdenville soon.

1935-12-20

Shirley Hudson born.

According to a report from Holdenville, Okla., Mr. and Mrs. Douglas Hudson are the proud parents of a baby girl, born recently.

1936-01-31

G. O. Bronstad Sr. is recuperating after a two weeks’ illness. . . .

1936-06-12

Mr. and Mrs. Douglas Hudson and daughters, Carolyn Sue and Shirley Ann of Holdenville, Okla., are visiting with relatives here. Mr. Hudson returned home Monday of this week. But Mrs. Hudson and children will visit here for some time.

1936-08-21

Mr. and Mrs. Otis Bronstad and children, Allen and Charlene, left Sunday morning for Holdenville, Oklahoma where they will visit Mr. and Mrs. Douglas Hudson and family. They will stop by Mr. and Mrs. Oscar Knudson’s home in Ada, Oklahoma, enroute. On the return trip they will visit Mr. and Mrs. Alton Tindall and family at Borger.

1936-11-20

Mrs. Douglas Hudson and children of Holdenville, Okla., arrived here last Tuesday to be at the bedside of Mrs. Hudson’s father, Mr. G. O. Bronstad, who has been very ill, but is some better at this time.

1937-05-14

Mrs. Douglas Hudson and daughters of Holdenville, Okla., are visiting relatives here.
